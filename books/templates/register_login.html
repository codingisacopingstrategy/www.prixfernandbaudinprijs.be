{% trans "Conditions for admission" %}

{% trans "An eligible book is one which has been published during the previous year, as well as:" %}

  • {% trans "a book created by a graphic designer/Artist designer working or living in Brussels for at least one year or a book to which an illustrator working or living in Brussels for at least one year has contributed;" %}
  • {% trans "a book, the publisher of which has his main office in Brussels ;" %}
  • {% trans "a book, the printer of which has his main work-place in Brussels." %}

{% trans "At least one of the three above-mentioned parties must be situated in the Brussels-Capital region or in Wallonia." %}

{% trans "Competing in the Fernand Baudin Prize is entirely free of charge." %}
